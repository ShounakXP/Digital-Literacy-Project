# Stay Safe Online Checklist

- Never click on suspicious links
- Verify website URLs before entering details
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Do not share OTPs with anyone
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions
- Always check sender email addresses

## UPI Safety
- Double-check UPI IDs before payment
- Never approve unknown payment requests

## Reporting
- Report at: cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930
